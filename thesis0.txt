BasicGame
  SpriteSet
    floor > Immovable img=newset/floor2 hidden=True
    wall > Immovable img=oryx/wall3 autotiling=True
    enemy > 
      enemy1 > Missile orientation=RIGHT speed=0.2 img=oryx/slime6
      enemy2 > Missile orientation=LEFT speed=0.2 img=oryx/slime1
    avatar  > MovingAvatar img=oryx/slime4
    goal  > Immovable img=oryx/slime2
  LevelMapping
    . > floor
    w > wall
    1 > floor enemy1
    2 > floor enemy2
    A > floor avatar
    g > floor goal
  InteractionSet
    avatar wall > stepBack
    avatar enemy > killSprite scoreChange=-1
    goal avatar  > killSprite scoreChange=2
    enemy wall > reverseDirection
    enemy enemy > stepBack
  TerminationSet
    SpriteCounter stype=goal    limit=0 win=True
    SpriteCounter stype=avatar win=False
